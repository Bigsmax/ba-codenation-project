ABOUT BEHSAT

Visual Storyteller/ UX Consultant

A top tier theatre practitioner, filmmaker, and dramaturge, with a strong reputation of creating live performances across diverse media on both sides of the Atlantic. His practice spans film, theatre, dance, scenography, and authoring for intermedial performance for live theatre and major events.

After 25 years of working in theatre, Behsat began training towards working as a UI/UX designer. His experience with programming and design began at CodeNation, where he was able to infuse his extensive knowledge and experience of visual storytelling to creating compelling digital projects that resonate with project missions, aims and goals. Prior to starting his journey at CodeNation Behsat founded his own acting studio in East London, The East London Academy of Dramatic Art where he created tailor made courses for creative performance practice. Behsat holds an M.F.A in Theater Directing from Columbia University in New York City and an MSc in Dance Science from Trinity Laban Conservatoire.
